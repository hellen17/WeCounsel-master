About Us

Ever felt the need to have someone to talk to when you feel depressed but unable to reach out to anyone? WeCounsel provides a safe space to connect you to online therapists of your choice as well as your fellow peers.

Pros of Online Therapy

Online therapy provides one with a strong sense of anonimity hence they feel they are in a much safe place to share there deep dark secrets that trouble them. Online therapy also gives those who live in remote areas a chance to contact a counsellor.
